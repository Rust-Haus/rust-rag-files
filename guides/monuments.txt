RUST MONUMENTS GUIDE
====================
Monuments are points of interest with loot and puzzles.

## EASY MONUMENTS (No cards needed)
- Supermarket: Food, components
- Gas Station: Fuel, components
- Mining Outpost: Recycler, refinery
- Fishing Village: Boats, fishing gear
- Lighthouse: Basic loot
- Oxum's Gas Station: Recycler access

## MEDIUM MONUMENTS (Green/Blue card)
- Harbor: Green card puzzle
- Sewer Branch: Green card puzzle
- Satellite Dish: Green card puzzle
- Water Treatment: Blue card puzzle
- Airfield: Blue card puzzle
- Trainyard: Blue card puzzle

## HARD MONUMENTS (Red card)
- Launch Site: Red card puzzle, Bradley APC
- Military Tunnels: Red card, elite crates
- Oil Rig (Small/Large): Blue card puzzle areas
- Underwater Labs: Some sections require keycards

## SAFE ZONES
- Outpost: No PvP, vending machines, recycler
- Bandit Camp: No PvP, gambling, recycler

For more details: https://www.corrosionhour.com/rust-monuments-guide/
