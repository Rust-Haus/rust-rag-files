RUST PVP COMBAT GUIDE
=====================
Combat skills are essential for survival.

## WEAPONS BY TIER
Early: Bow, Crossbow, Eoka, Waterpipe
Mid: Revolver, Semi-Auto Pistol, Thompson, Custom SMG
Late: AK-47, LR-300, M249, Bolt Action

## COMBAT TIPS
- Headshots deal 2x damage
- Crouching improves accuracy
- Strafe while shooting
- Use cover constantly
- Reload behind cover

## ARMOR PRIORITY
- Head: Metal Facemask or Coffee Can
- Chest: Metal Chest Plate or Road Sign
- Legs: Road Sign Kilt
- Hazmat suit for radiation areas

## HEALING
- Bandages: Slow heal over time
- Syringes: Instant 15 HP, fast craft
- Med Kits: Full heal but slow
- Food: Passive healing when full
