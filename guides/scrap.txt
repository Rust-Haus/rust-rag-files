RUST SCRAP FARMING GUIDE
========================
Scrap is the main research currency in Rust.

## SCRAP SOURCES
- Barrels: 2-5 scrap each
- Crates: 10-25 scrap each
- Recycling components: Major source
- Gambling at Bandit Camp
- Selling items at Outpost/Bandit

## BEST RECYCLER ITEMS
- Tech Trash: 20 scrap
- CCTV Camera: 25 scrap
- Targeting Computer: 25 scrap
- Rifle Body: 25 scrap
- SMG Body: 15 scrap
- Semi-Auto Body: 15 scrap
- Road Signs: 5 scrap
- Sheet Metal: 8 scrap

## EFFICIENT ROUTES
- Road runs: Follow roads, hit barrels
- Underwater Labs: Dense loot, requires diving gear
- Oil Rig: High tier but dangerous
- Cargo Ship: Moving event, good loot
