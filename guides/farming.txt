RUST FARMING GUIDE
==================
Farming provides food and crafting materials.

## CROPS
- Corn: Food, can craft Tea
- Pumpkin: Food, hydration
- Hemp: Cloth for armor/meds
- Potatoes: Food, corn alternative
- Berries: Quick food source

## SETUP
- Water: Connect to water source or use sprinklers
- Light: Natural sunlight or grow lights
- Planters: Large planter holds 9 plants
- Genetics: Crossbreed for better yields

## GENETICS (YHGW)
- Y: Yield (more per harvest)
- H: Hardiness (survives longer)
- G: Growth speed
- W: Water efficiency
- X: Empty slot (bad)

## OPTIMAL GENES
- GGGGGG or YYYYYY for single focus
- Mix of Y and G for balance
- Remove X genes through crossbreeding
