RUST BEGINNER'S GUIDE
=====================
Welcome to Rust! This guide covers the basics for new players.

## FIRST STEPS
1. Gather resources: Hit trees for wood, pick up stones and hemp
2. Craft a Stone Hatchet and Stone Pickaxe
3. Build a small starter base (2x1 or 2x2)
4. Place a Tool Cupboard (TC) and sleeping bag inside

## ESSENTIAL EARLY ITEMS
- Sleeping Bag: Respawn point
- Tool Cupboard: Prevents others from building near your base
- Wooden Door + Lock: Secure your base
- Campfire: Cook food and stay warm
- Furnace: Smelt metal ore

## SURVIVAL TIPS
- Stay hydrated and fed (check health, hunger, thirst bars)
- Avoid cold at night - stay near fire or wear clothes
- Watch for radiation near monuments
- PvP is common - trust no one
